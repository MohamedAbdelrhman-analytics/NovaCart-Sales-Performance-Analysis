# NovaCart Sales Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes sales performance for a retail company using Excel, SQL Server, and Power BI.

## 🎯 Business Problem

The company noticed that revenue was growing while profit growth was slowing down. The goal was to identify the main drivers of revenue and profit, evaluate branches, sellers, products, and customers, and provide actionable recommendations.

## 📊 Analysis Performed

## 💡 Key Insights

Electronics category generated the highest revenue and profit.

Some products showed high revenue but relatively low profit margins.

Revenue and profit declined toward the end of the analyzed period.

Seller performance varied significantly across branches.

## ✅ Recommendations

Focus marketing efforts on high-profit categories.

Review pricing strategy for low-margin products.

Investigate the causes of the recent revenue decline.

Develop retention campaigns for inactive customers.
